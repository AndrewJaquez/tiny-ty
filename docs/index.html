Chrome Extension

tiny-yt

Open the current YouTube video in a small Picture-in-Picture window that stays on top while you work.

Privacy Policy View Repository

What it does

Click the toolbar icon on a YouTube video page to open Picture-in-Picture. Click again to close it.

Permissions

tiny-yt uses only activeTab and scripting. It does not use analytics, remote code, or background network requests.

Privacy

The extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Full details are on the privacy policy page.
